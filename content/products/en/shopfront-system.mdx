# Commercial Shopfront System

Create stunning retail environments with our versatile shopfront glazing system. Designed for maximum product visibility while meeting Australian commercial building codes.

## System Benefits

### Visual Impact
- Minimalist 50mm sightlines
- Floor-to-ceiling glass capability
- Seamless corner connections available

### Security Options
- Standard toughened glass (P1A)
- Laminated glass upgrade (P2A-P5A)
- Roller shutter integration
- After-hours grille compatibility

### Accessibility
- AS 1428.1 compliant door options
- Automatic door operator ready
- Low threshold for easy access

## Door Configurations

### Swing Doors
- Single or double leaf
- 90° or 180° opening
- Floor spring or overhead closer

### Sliding Doors
- Automatic or manual operation
- Bi-parting configuration
- Emergency breakout option

### Pivot Doors
- Dramatic entrance statements
- Panels up to 1500mm wide
- Top and bottom pivot hardware

- Retail stores and boutiques
- Shopping center tenancies
- Bank branches
- Restaurant frontages
- Office building lobbies
- Showrooms

- Thermally improved frames
- Drainage provisions
- Adjustable threshold
- Concealed fixings
- Weather sealing
