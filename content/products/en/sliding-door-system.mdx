# Premium Sliding Door System

Transform your living space with our premium sliding door system, designed specifically for Australian homes. Experience seamless indoor-outdoor flow with panels that glide effortlessly on precision-engineered tracks.

## Design Features

### Slim Sightlines
Our engineered profiles maximize glass area while maintaining structural integrity, providing unobstructed views to your outdoor spaces.

### Smooth Operation
- Twin ball-bearing rollers rated for 100,000+ cycles
- Self-leveling adjustment for perfect alignment
- Soft-close option available

### Security
- Multi-point locking as standard
- Keyed alike option for multiple doors
- Meets AS 5039 security requirements

## Performance

### Thermal Efficiency
Thermally broken aluminum frames combined with double glazing achieve excellent energy ratings, keeping your home comfortable year-round.

### Weather Sealing
- Triple-seal weather strip system
- Positive pressure drainage
- Tested to 300Pa water resistance

### Bushfire Protection
BAL-29 rated configuration available for bushfire-prone areas, featuring tempered glass and metal seals.
